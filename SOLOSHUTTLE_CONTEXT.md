# 🏸 SOLOSHUTTLE PROJECT CONTEXT

## Project Name
SoloShuttle

## Vision

SoloShuttle is a premium AI-powered badminton solo training app.

The goal is NOT to build another badminton app.

The goal is to build the world's best badminton coach for players training without a partner.

Every feature must help the player improve.

The app should feel like having a professional badminton coach in your pocket.

1. Analyze project.

2. Explain your plan.

3. Wait for approval.

# Coaching Philosophy

Content should follow professional badminton coaching principles.

Use structured progressions.

Every drill should include:

• Objective

• Duration

• Difficulty

• Benefits

• Coaching Cues

• Common Mistakes

• Progression

Do not generate generic badminton content.
