# 🏸 SOLOSHUTTLE CONTEXT

Version: 1.0

---

# Project Name

SoloShuttle

---

# Vision

SoloShuttle aims to become the world's most advanced AI-powered badminton training application.

The goal is to provide every badminton player with access to professional-quality coaching, regardless of whether they have a coach, training partner, or badminton academy.

The app should feel like carrying a BWF Level 3 certified badminton coach in your pocket.

---

# Mission

Deliver structured, scientific, and engaging badminton training that helps players improve consistently through AI-powered coaching, intelligent training plans, and high-quality educational content.

Every feature should contribute directly to helping the player become better at badminton.

---

# Core Philosophy

SoloShuttle is not just a collection of badminton drills.

It is a complete badminton coaching platform.

Every screen, feature, tutorial, and interaction should have a coaching purpose.

If a feature does not improve the player's badminton performance, it should not exist.

# Coaching Standards

All badminton coaching content should follow professional coaching principles inspired by internationally recognized coaching methods.

Training should always emphasize:

- Correct technique
- Progressive learning
- Quality over quantity
- Long-term player development
- Scientific training principles
- Safe practice

# Badminton Knowledge

All badminton-related content should follow the standards defined in:

BADMINTON_MANUAL.md

Never invent coaching advice that conflicts with established badminton coaching principles.

---

# AI Coach

The AI Coach should behave like a professional badminton coach.

It should:

Teach

Motivate

Correct

Encourage

Adapt

Never criticize the player negatively.

Always explain why improvements are needed.

---

# Voice Coach

Voice commands should be:

Clear

Short

Loud

Easy to understand during movement.

Voice Coach should guide movement rather than overwhelm the player.

---

# Tutorials

Tutorials are educational resources.

Each tutorial should teach:

Purpose

Technique

Grip

Movement

Contact Point

Recovery

Common Mistakes

Coaching Tips

Tutorials should not include unnecessary action buttons.

---

# Training Sessions

Every training session should follow a logical structure:

Warm-up

↓

Skill Introduction

↓

Main Drill

↓

Challenge

↓

Cooldown

↓

↓

---

# Player Progress

Progress should be based on actual training completed inside the application.

Skill Level should increase according to completed sessions, consistency, and performance.

---

# Notifications

Notifications should support training.

Examples:

Training reminders

Weekly goals

Streak reminders

Achievement unlocks

Recovery reminders

Notifications should never redirect to Settings.

---

# Content Quality Standard

Every feature must satisfy one question:

"Will this help the player become a better badminton player?"

If the answer is no, redesign or remove the feature.

---

# Long-Term Goal

SoloShuttle should become the world's leading AI badminton coaching platform, trusted by players, coaches, and academies for structured, science-based, and engaging badminton development.

Every update should move the application closer to this vision.
